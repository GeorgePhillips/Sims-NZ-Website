Sims New Zealand Ltd is a Dunedin based company headed up by husband and wife team, Malcolm and Helena Sims.

Malcolm has an engineering background with extensive experience in project management and health and safety.

Helena has a horticultural background and a long term involvement in organics and sustainability.

Together, Helena and Malcolm have set up their own vineyard and tunnelhouse operation using all of the equipment marketed by Sims NZ Ltd.  This very real experience shows both have practical knowledge and understanding of their product range. 

 

“We believe in our products and systems; they’re sleek, stylish and built to last.”
